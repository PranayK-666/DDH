Delhi Design Hackathon (DDH) is Delhiâ€™s biggest overnight UI/UX Design Hackathon which witnesses participants from all over India every year during the Annual Techfest of Delhi Technological University.

What is DDH?

During the event, school/college students team up and collaborate on Designing creative solutions to the given problem statement in a limited time frame and then present the final solution to the judges.

While designing, participants follow a complete UX process to collect data and analyse the problem to provide a creative solution. Some of the methods include building User Personas, Journey Maps, User flow etc.

Why Attend DDH?

The event ends with presenting a high-fidelity Prototype of the solution to our judges and the winners are decided by the jury panel after evaluating all the deliverables. Through DDH, we wish to provide exposure to young designers and uplift the whole UI/UX Design Community.

It would be an honor for us to be able to associate with you through DDH. With your esteemed support, we are sure to add glory to the grandeur of Delhi Design Hackathon.
